What Are These?

These are a collection of vent poems that I write whenever I feel the need. None of them are meant to be that good. Really, none of them are meant to be read but here they are.

Shit:

    I feast on my own shit
    Like maggots feasting on a new carcass
    Isolated from everyone else
    As they eat their shares of the hunt
    Poised, I grab a handful
    And nibble it
    I grab another handful
    And devour it
    The consistency is making me sick
    I swallow another mouthful
    It’s disgusting
    Like a bitter mud, but somehow more chunky
    I can feel the remnants of what I ate prior
    I gag as it swishes around in my mouth
    I try to open my throat
    I try not to think about it
    Clear my head
    But all I can think about is how they despised me
    How they turned their backs on me
    How they treat me like an animal
    I don’t want to think about that
    So I just think about the shit
    How it smells
    How it smudges my fingertips
    How it sticks to my teeth
    I don’t stop
    Handful by handful I continue to consume
    My own fecal content rentering me
    To start the cycle once more
    I’m halfway done now
    Yet I still feel hungry
    And so, as a method to feel satiated
    I grab the rest of it with both hands
    I then slam my face towards it, mouth agape
    But it doesn’t work
    I just can’t do it
    My body doesn’t let me
    Shit covering me face, filling every pore, I lie down
    I begin to feel nauseated
    And expel everything I had just dined on
    All of my hard work now on the ground
    Gone to waste
    I have no energy to get up
    And, with the taste of shit in my mouth
    I let sleep take me forever
    The last thing I remember being my final supper
    As I rot on the ground

Ultimatum:

    I get antsy when she gets mad 
    It’s like there’s butterflies in my stomach
    But they’re not feeding off any nectar 
    They’re squashed flat on the ground
    Decaying

    She needs to know what that feels like
    She refuses to acknowledge me in the mirror
    Always avoiding eye contact
    Always with her arms crossed 
    Blank expression

    One day I snapped
    I gave myself an ultimatum
    And I chose wrong
    At least that’s what she thought
    I can’t remember anything else about her

All Gone:

	
    Eyes crossed
    So far I can see into my brain
    I don’t like it
    It’s the memories of pleasure that capture us forever

    I wanted it to all go away
    I began by banging my head against the metal
    And waited until I felt something
    It took a bit, but I got there
    Messy, but I got there

    Next I went for the eyes
    The grapes of wrath
    Those grotesque balls of nerve endings and fluids
    That allowed me to see what I hated
    I couldn’t take it anymore
    I took them

    But my hatred was still there
    My body was still connected to this world
    For I still had a feeling
    Barely one, but a feeling still
    I wanted it gone
    I wanted it all gone.

Her Father

    The chill in my breath reminds her of her father
    Suffering quietly in his chair
    Lips pursed to another cigarette
    So he can’t remember his name
    Passionately scratching at a scab on his arm
    Until it starts to bleed
    Red streaming down to his elbow
    Puddling onto the carpet
    Another stain.

From Rebirth to Rebirth:

    Poor hot wax onto my skin
    Make me into your statue 
    Your muse
    Something you can gawk at and criticize
    And then one day when I awaken
    I can look back on myself
    And remember how naive I was
    How I thought that I would be reborn
    And come back as something that mattered
    Instead I got stuck being me.

How I Will Die:

    When she touches me I feel like I’m floating 
    Across the dead sky
    Storming and unforgiving
    I couldn’t ask for anything better
    I know how I will die 
    And it will be because of her

    Her touch will kill me
    Infest my skin with worms
    That gnaw at my tissue 
    Until there’s nothing left
    Until not even God will recognize me
    Exposing my nerves for everyone to see
    Anything for a perfectionist like me

Desire

    Bite my lip and rip it off
    At least until it starts to bleed 
    Tear off the dead skin
    Feed it back to me

    Quit while you’re ahead
    Find out how much I know about you
    Let me in
    Let me see you
    Let me become you
    Encapsulate every part of you
    Melt my body into yours
    Like hot wax does
    Let me wear your skin
    Your hair
    See through your eyes
    I want to feel everything you feel
    Know everything you know
    You and I will be inseparable 
    And maybe then I could love you

Let Me Go:

 	
    Disfigured and brutalized
    My own disguise melts at my command
    I am a shadow of my former self
    Disgustingly mistaken
    Put me on ice to die

    Wander around the walls
    Paint them in someone’s blood
    You’ll have to decide whose

    Quaking innocent children
    Sing songs for the deceased
    Yet no one will sing for me
    I’m alright with that

    The medication is wearing off 
    I’m beginning to feel it again
    Take it away doc
    I don’t want this for myself
    Give it to someone else
    Give me something to mask it
    Someone else’s hand to hold
    Someone to call my own
    My own personal me

This Is About You:

    I hope to one day be forgotten
    So they’ll defile my image no longer
    But I doubt it’ll ever happen
    Information is slow but still travels faster than itself

    Without my muse I am nothing
    But I somehow still found a way
    Now I stand to take yours away
    So I can be whole again

    I hope they don’t remember me after today
    I hope you cry at the mention of my name
    I want you to feel the sense of dread that I’ve felt
    I need you to understand where I’m coming from
    This isn’t about you
    None of it is
    But it’s not about me either

Go Away:

    Go away. 
    I didn’t ask for you to be here.
    To annex my private thoughts.
    And plaster them around for all to see.

    I gave you the hints.
    I hope you understood them.
    I don’t want you to be shocked at what comes next.

    Wrongfully committed.
    Vindicated by further pleasures.
    Beyond our comprehension.
    Passion tears us apart.
    In the heat of paradise.

    Go away.
    I don’t want you around.
    Nobody does.
