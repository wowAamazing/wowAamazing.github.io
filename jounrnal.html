July 20th, 2026

This entry is meant to be more of a proof of concept rather than anything else. It fills space. It makes the website feel more "lived-in" than it really is. Really, not much should go here; there is emphasis on the phrase "careful selection" as journals, in my eyes, are meant to be a private thing. If I wanted to talk about my personal life in gruesome detail and have everyone know then there must be something wrong with me. I guess a case can be made for the idea of screaming into the void but I am not here to do that.

The whole point of this page is to act as a form of motivation so that I actually come back to it and work on it rather than leaving it to collect dust. Maybe it would be better to call this a "Blog," but I really have no idea what the difference would be.

- Daria
